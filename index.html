Dimla Rani Brinda Rani Government High School

Dimla Rani Brinda Rani Government High School also known as Dimla RBR Govt. High School. It offers classes upto Secondary level. The school is located at Dimla Upazila in Nilphamari District, Bangladesh. The school was established in 1917. Md. Abdur Razzak is currently the head teacher of the school.
